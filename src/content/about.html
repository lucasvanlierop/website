About me

I'm Lucas, a freelance software engineer, working professionaly since 2004. My goal is to build well designed, well tested and properly documented applications that exceed your expectations.

I work mostly in PHP and Javascript, but I'm learning other languages as well. For more info check my expertise.

Besides being engineer I'm also organizer of DomCode, a non-profit foundation with 1200+ members that organizes events like the annual DomCode conference and monthly DomCode meetups where (inter)national speakers share their knowledge on software development. Since I value knowledge sharing a lot I became an occasional speaker myself.

I love to work with other freelancers or companies. Contact me if you are interested.
